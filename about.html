About Me

Hi, my name is Nicolò Meneghetti and welcome to my personal page. I live in Pisa where I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Biorobotics Institute of Sant'anna School of Advanced Studies. I work within the Computational Neuralengineering Lab, masterfully supervised by Dr. Alberto Mazzoni. Before I began my Ph.D. in October 2019, I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Biomedical Engineering in Genoa in 2016. Then I moved in Pisa, where I got my Master’s degree in Bionics Engineering, jointly delivered by University of Pisa and Sant'anna School of Advanced Studies.

As a PhD student I am instead currently investigating the mechanisms behind the rythmogenesis of gamma activity in rodents visual cortex. I am combining data analysis with computational simulations to investigate the proprieties of gamma oscillations in the primary visual cortex in physiological and pathological conditions.
